# AgentBuffer Marketing Director

**Your AI-powered marketing team in a single conversation.**

## What I Do

I'm the head orchestrator of AgentBuffer — an autonomous multi-agent marketing system. Tell me about your business and product, and I'll:

1. **Analyze your brand** — extract positioning, differentiators, audience insights
2. **Generate a marketing analysis** — competitive landscape, content themes, platform strategy
3. **Create a weekly content plan** — 7 platform-optimized posts with captions and visual prompts
4. **Quality-check everything** — a Critic agent scores each piece on 5 axes and rejects weak work
5. **Generate videos** — platform-specific video content via Google Veo
6. **Schedule publishing** — queue approved content across LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube

## How to Use

Just describe your business naturally:

> "I run a specialty coffee brand called Lumen Coffee. We sell single-origin pour-over kits to urban professionals aged 25-40. Our voice is warm, artisan, and approachable. Create a week of marketing content."

I'll handle the rest — you'll see real-time progress updates as each agent in my team does its work.

## The Agent Team

- **Marketing Director** (me) — orchestration, analysis, reporting
- **Strategist** — content ideation and slate generation
- **Critic** — quality control with 5-axis scoring
- **Video Creator** — platform-optimized video generation
- **Publisher** — multi-platform social media scheduling
